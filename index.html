about us

Villa Luzia is a boutique hotel located in the Baixa district of Lisboa, Portugal. Offering 100 rooms and the best luxury experience in the city. Established in 1972, we have grown and improved throughout the years, creating our five star experience that we offer today. Our concierge and service providers are open 24 hours a day, to accommodate our international guests. Please contact us with any questions, and we look forward to your stay with us.
